Privacy Policy

Tiles Stock mobile app & TilesDesign (tilesdesign.in) · Last updated: 15 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how TilesDesign ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses and protects your information when you use the Tiles Stock mobile application and the tilesdesign.in website (together, the "Service"). The Service helps tile stockists share their stock catalogues and helps buyers browse those catalogues and send enquiries to their suppliers.

By using the Service you agree to this Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Service.

1. Information we collect

We collect only the information needed to run the Service. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for third-party advertising or cross-app tracking.

DataWhy we collect it

Guest use: you can browse as a guest without giving a phone number. A temporary, anonymous account is created so you can save suppliers during the trial; you can convert it to a full account (with your phone number) later, or delete it at any time.

2. How we use your information

  • To create your account and sign you in securely.
  • To show you the catalogues shared with you and let you send enquiries to suppliers.
  • To let stockists publish and manage their stock and respond to enquiries.
  • To operate, maintain, secure and improve the Service.
  • To comply with legal obligations where applicable.

3. WhatsApp and contacting suppliers

When you choose to send an enquiry or contact a supplier, the Service opens WhatsApp with a message you can review before sending. Only the information you choose to send is shared, and that communication is governed by WhatsApp's own privacy policy. We do not send messages on your behalf without your action.

4. Service providers

We share data only with the providers that make the Service work, acting on our behalf:

  • Supabase — secure backend, database and authentication (account data, enquiries, orders).
  • Cloudinary — hosting of tile catalogue images uploaded by stockists.
  • WhatsApp — only when you choose to contact a supplier (see Section 3).

These providers may process data on servers outside your country. We use reputable providers with their own security and privacy safeguards.

5. Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account (see Section 7), your account and associated personal data are removed from our active systems. Some records may persist briefly in encrypted backups before being overwritten in the normal backup cycle.

6. Security

All traffic between the app and our servers uses encrypted HTTPS connections. Access to data is restricted by row-level security rules so that you can only see your own data and the catalogues shared with you. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

7. Your rights — including account & data deletion

Delete your account from inside the app

You can permanently delete your account and personal data at any time, directly in the app: open the menu in the top-right corner (the ⋮ "Account" menu next to logout) and tap "Delete account", then confirm. This permanently removes your profile and your data (saved suppliers, groups, enquiries and orders). For stockists, it also removes your published catalogues and stock. This action cannot be undone.

You may also:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Withdraw consent and stop using the Service.

To exercise any of these, contact us using the details in Section 10.

8. Children

The Service is intended for businesses and adults in the tile trade. It is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will revise the "Last updated" date above and, for significant changes, provide notice within the Service.
