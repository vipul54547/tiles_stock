# Tiles Stock — TestFlight Beta: Tester Notes

Thank you for testing the **Tiles Stock** beta! This is an early version — please
try the steps below and tell us anything that looks wrong, confusing, or slow.

## How to install
1. Install the free **TestFlight** app from the App Store.
2. Open the invite link we sent you (on WhatsApp/email) and tap **Accept** → **Install**.
3. Open **Tiles Stock**.

## Important to know first
- **Login by phone OTP is not live yet** in this beta. Please use **Guest / Browse**
  to test — you can do almost everything without logging in.
- This is a beta, so some data may be reset. Don't rely on it for real business yet.
- The app shows **no prices** anywhere — that is intentional, not a bug.

---

## What to test (a quick 10-minute pass)

### Buyers (dealers browsing stock)
- [ ] App opens and you can browse tile designs as a guest.
- [ ] Open a design → you see the photo, size, finish, **Boxes Available** (and **no price**).
- [ ] Search works (try a colour or size, e.g. "white", "600x600").
- [ ] Add a design to **My Choice**, then send an enquiry — WhatsApp opens with a message.
- [ ] Add a supplier by their link (if you were given one) — it appears under My Suppliers.

### Stockists (if you were given a stockist test login)
- [ ] Dashboard shows your stock; the stats bar shows **Designs** and **Boxes** (no value/price).
- [ ] Add a design → there is **no price field** (correct).
- [ ] Share a stock-list link and open it on another phone.

### Everyone
- [ ] On the login screen, **"Don't have your login yet?"** → "Get my login details"
      opens WhatsApp with a pre-filled message.
- [ ] Tapping a `tilesdesign.in/s/...` link opens the app (not just the browser).
- [ ] Account menu (⋮, top-right) → **"Delete account"** is present.
      ⚠️ Only actually delete on a throwaway test account — it is permanent.
- [ ] Camera / photo upload works (stockists).

## How to report a problem
For anything you find, please send us (on WhatsApp **+91 97269 66906** or
**tilesfinders@gmail.com**):
1. **What you did** (the steps).
2. **What happened** vs **what you expected**.
3. A **screenshot** if possible, and your **phone model + iOS version**.

You can also use TestFlight's built-in feedback: take a screenshot inside the app
and TestFlight will ask if you want to send it to us.

Thank you — your feedback shapes the app. 🙏
